ID Cards

WANZ ID cards cost $5.00 per paddler. (Fees may increase around Sprint Nationals.)

No ID card, no paddle. These are required for all Waka Ama regattas.

Email the following to info@rbocc.co.nz:

  • Headshot photo
  • Proof of ID (Passport preferred, or driver's license/birth certificate)
Send these items as soon as possible to avoid delays in registration.

Responsibilities

As a club member, you're expected to:

  • Foster the visions, values, and objectives of the club
  • Maintain the integrity of the Club at all times
  • Commit to your team and be a team player
  • Treat all club equipment with care and report damages
  • Abide by safety rules and regulations
  • Treat all members with courtesy and respect
  • Participate in social hui events
  • Assist in fundraising activities
  • Pay membership fees on time
