Your Membership Supports More Than You Think

When you become a member, you're not just joining a club — you're helping us keep the kaupapa alive. Membership fees help us maintain waka, fund youth programs, support local events, and preserve the cultural integrity of Waka Ama in Aotearoa.

Every dollar goes back into our paddlers, our gear, and our whānau. Thank you for being part of our journey.
